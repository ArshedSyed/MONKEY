History

Lamborghini is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini. The company's headquarters are located in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy.

Models

Lamborghini has produced several iconic models over the years, including:

  • Aventador
  • Huracan
  • Gallardo
  • Murcielago
